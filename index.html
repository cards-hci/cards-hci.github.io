Our Mission Statement

We are an HCI research group at Oberlin College. Our enthusiastic lab is composed of undergraduate students and external collaborators and is led by lab director Professor Emily. We are broadly interested in design and the study of interfaces for communication work, learning, and play. Take a gander at what we're about!
